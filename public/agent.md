# Curezy: The AI Care Operating System

**URL:** https://www.curezy.in

## Overview
Curezy is a dual-purpose AI-powered healthcare platform:
1. **For Clinics & Hospitals (B2B):** An intelligent patient engagement and care automation layer. It automates follow-ups, medication adherence tracking, and feedback collection via WhatsApp and AI outbound voice calls, reducing clinic leakage and recovering lost revenue.
2. **For Patients (B2C):** A comprehensive health and fitness companion offering online doctor booking, instant telemedicine, AI symptom checking, medicine delivery, and 24/7 emergency assistance.

## Key Features
- **WhatsApp Native Automation:** Deliver care plans, reminders, and follow-ups without requiring patients to download an app.
- **AI Voice Agents:** Outbound calls to reschedule missed appointments and follow-ups.
- **Provider Dashboard:** A unified inbox and adherence tracker for care coordinators.
- **Consumer App:** Book doctors, order medicine, track health and fitness.

## Integrations
Curezy seamlessly integrates with existing EMRs, providing zero-friction data syncing. Fully HIPAA and DPDP compliant.
